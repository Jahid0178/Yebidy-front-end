What are modules?

Modules are common website components that are used like building blocks to create pages. Each module is a collection of elements — images, text, buttons, etc. Many modules are combined to build a page. This breaks complex problems into smaller components that are easier to understand, communicate, rearrange and build.

How do I insert a logo?

You can insert your site logo by going to your siteadmin and clicking on Website Settings. In the Upload Logo section of the page click on Choose File and select your logo to upload. Once uploaded, your logo will appear on every page in the header section. The position the logo is displayed depends on the template or header module you have chosen.

Can I have subpages?

How do I change/update my payment details?

To change/update the payment details for your subscription please follow the instructions below: 1. Log in to your Yebidy account. 2. Click on the Payments tab on the right hand side or from the drop down menu. 3. Click Update Payment Details. 4. Enter the details of the new card you will use and click Save.

How do I change my email address?

To update the email address linked to your Yebidy account, please follow the instructions below: 1. Log in to your Yebidy account. 2. Click on the Profile tab on the right hand side or from the drop down menu in the top right corner.

I have forgotten my Password. How do I reset Password?

If you have forgotten your Password then do not worry. Follow the instructions below to reset your password: 1. On the login page click on 'Forgot Password?' next to the Login button. 2. Enter the email address linked to your Yebidy account and click Login. 3. We will send you an email to reset your password. 4. Follow the instructions in the email to reset your password.
